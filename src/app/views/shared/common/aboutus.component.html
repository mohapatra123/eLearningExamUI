FinTechEdu

A growing Fintech and Edutech Platform aimed for excellence in the field of fintech and edutech. FinTechEdu through one of its initiative stives to explore every possible opportunity in the field of Financial Literacy and Financial Education.

We are a team of Senior Bankers, Academician, Journalist and Expert in Information Technology having vast experience in their respective fields and using their knowledge to create an ecosystem where financial inclusion, skill development, technology enablement and Employment generation can be areas where all the efforts will be done to fulfil the objective.

FinTechEdu is based out of Bhopal, started its operations in 2021 and continuing doing impactful work in the field of financial inclusion in sync with the RBIs vision and working towards digital transformation and technology training areas.

OUR MISSION

To make finance and technology education an integral part of learning, that is affordable, accessible and flexible for anyone irrespective of their background.

OUR VISION

We envision the best learning experience for anyone who wants to be empowered to change their life through education.

OUR VALUES

We believe in the following values:
  • To continually pursue innovation and novelty in learning and try to achieve equality of opportunity by making sure learning is devoid of any societal barriers
  • To develop a sense of empathy for the community of learners and engage in the habit of giving back to the community
